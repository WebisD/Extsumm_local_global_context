the prevalence of anxiety and depression was significantly lower in students of the third year compared to other grades ( p < 0.05 ) .
the students were interviewed for addiction , generalized anxiety disorder , and major depressive disorder according to the dsm - iv criteria . 

the prevalence of depression was significantly higher in students with lower economic status ( p < 0.05 ) .
the prevalence of anxiety was significantly higher in the students who used the internet for chatting , amusement ( such as downloading music , films , and pictures ) , and reading the news compared to the students who used the internet for other reasons ( p < 0.05 ) .
