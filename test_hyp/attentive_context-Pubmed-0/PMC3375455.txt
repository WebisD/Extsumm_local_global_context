we report in this paper an outbreak of dermatitis in hedgehogs caused by c. tripilis with description of keys for the identification of the family psoroptidae and the genus caparinia .
in february 2010 , dermatitis characterized by scale and self - trauma due to pruritus was recognized in a group of 22 four - toed hedgehogs ( a. albiventris wagner , 1841 ) from a local pet shop in gwangju , korea .
